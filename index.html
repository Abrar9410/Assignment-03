Explore Our Latest Work

Our Latest Portfolio

Here are some of our latest ongoing projects. We showcase our new projects so that you can get an idea what we are capable of.

MODERN DETACHED

A contemporary, respectful new home inserted comfortably to a suburban street. The project explores light, depth and geometry of threshold. Inside, large areas of glazing and windows keep the spaces bright and airy. The central staircase is a play of light and shadows. The stair void also enhances the spaciousness of the house by creating a three-storey space referencing the Arts and Crafts aesthetic with a predominant parti, street exposure and a central hall with access to rooms.

COVE RIDGE

A new build home composed of zenith light and long views of the horizon. Oversized windows throughout, a huge zenith skylight & full-height glass doors create an inside/outside threshold and allow house to flood with light.
